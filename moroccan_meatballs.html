moroccan meatballs

  • 500g lamb mince
  • onion finely cut
  • 1 egg
  • 2 teaspoons garlic
  • 1/4 cup mint + parsley finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon corriander
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • breadcrumbs

directions:

  1. combine all meatball ingredients into one bowl.
  2. roll meatball ingredients into 2cm size balls and fry.
