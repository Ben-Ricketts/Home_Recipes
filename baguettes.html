baguettes

  • 1, 1/2 cups warm water
  • 1, 1/2 yeast
  • 2 teaspoon sugar
  • 3, 1/4 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt

directions:

  1. cut down the middle.
  2. let rise for 30 minutes.
  3. bake at 200*c-220*c for 15 minutes
