sourdough bread

  • 460g flour
  • 330ml water
  • 150g starter
  • 1 teaspoon salt

directions:

  1. feed starter
  2. autolyse, hydrate flour. shaggy dough. cover and leave for 3-4 hours
  3. add 150g starter and salt, wet hands to mix mix, put onto glass dish, leave for 30 minutes.
  4. start coil folds with wet hands every 30 minutes, for 3 hours (6 times in total)
  5. dough is soft and silky, should fall from dish. shape the dough, braid, roll on bench to create surface tension.
  6. put in floured bonaton, in fridge 8 hours or overnight.
  7. 20-30minutes in over with lid on, then another 15-20 minutes with lid off. 230*c.
