cookie time cookies

  • 225g of butter
  • 200g brown sugar
  • 100g castor sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 180g flour
  • 180g self-raising
  • 1 teaspoon cornflour
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 350g chocolate (best is whitakers)
  • makes 10-12

directions:

with a stand mixer, mix butter and sugar for at least 5 minutes. add eggs one at a time. then add all of the other ingredients. add the chocolate last. spoon the mixture into large mounds. then freeze for at least 30 minutes. bake at 180*c-200*c for 10-14 minutes
