cookie time cookies

225g butter

200g brown sugar

100g castor sugar

2 eggs

180g flour

180g self-raising

1 tsp cornflour

1.2 tsp salt

1 tsp baking soda

350g chocolate (best with whittakers)

makes 10-12

bake at 180c-200c for 10-14 minutes

with a stand mixer, mix butter and sugar for at least 5mins.
add eggs one at a time, then add all of the other ingredients.
add chocolate the last.
spoon mix into large mounds. then freeze for at least 30mins.
